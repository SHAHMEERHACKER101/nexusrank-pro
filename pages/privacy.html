Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 25, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our AI SEO toolkit services:

  • Text content you submit for processing
  • Usage analytics and performance metrics
  • Technical information like IP address and browser type
  • Pro account credentials (if applicable)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Processing your text through AI services
  • Tracking usage limits for free users
  • Improving our service quality and performance
  • Providing customer support when needed

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data is transmitted using HTTPS encryption
  • Text content is processed temporarily and not permanently stored
  • Usage data is stored locally in your browser
  • We use secure cloud infrastructure with enterprise-grade protection

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • DeepSeek AI: For processing your text content
  • Cloudflare: For hosting and security services
  • Patreon: For Pro subscription management

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local browser storage to:

  • Track your free usage limits
  • Remember your Pro login status
  • Improve your user experience

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who help us operate our platform

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection
  • Request data portability

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
