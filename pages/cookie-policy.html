Cookie Policy

Last updated: August 25, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can both make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

NexusRank Pro uses cookies and similar technologies (like local storage) to enhance your experience and provide better functionality.

How We Use Cookies and Local Storage

Essential Functionality

We use local browser storage to:

  • Track your daily usage limits for free tools
  • Remember your Pro login status
  • Save your preferences and settings
  • Maintain application state across sessions

Performance and Analytics

We may use cookies to:

  • Analyze how our website is used
  • Monitor website performance and functionality
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Understand user behavior to improve our services

Service Worker

We use a service worker to enable offline functionality and improve loading performance. This technology caches certain resources locally on your device.

Types of Data Stored

Local Storage Data:

  • nexusrank_usage - Usage tracking for free tools
  • nexusrank_pro - Pro subscription status

Session Data:

  • Current tool selections and inputs
  • UI preferences and settings
  • Temporary processing states

Third-Party Cookies

Our website may include content from third-party services that may set their own cookies:

Cloudflare

Provides security, performance, and analytics services. May set cookies for security and performance optimization.

Patreon

Handles Pro subscriptions and may set cookies when you interact with subscription features.

Font and Asset CDNs

Google Fonts and other CDNs may set cookies for performance optimization and analytics.

Managing Cookies

Browser Settings

You can control and/or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View which cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete cookies individually or all at once
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies entirely

Clear NexusRank Pro Data

To clear all data stored by NexusRank Pro:

  1. Open your browser's developer tools (F12)
  2. Navigate to the "Application" or "Storage" tab
  3. Find "Local Storage" and select our domain
  4. Delete the nexusrank_usage and nexusrank_pro entries

Important Note

Blocking or deleting cookies may affect the functionality of our website. Some features may not work properly without local storage.

Cookie Retention

Different types of data have different retention periods:

  • Usage Data: Resets every 24 hours automatically
  • Pro Status: Persists until manually cleared or logout
  • Session Data: Cleared when browser session ends
  • Cache Data: Managed by service worker, periodically updated

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect data stored locally:

  • Data is stored only in your browser's secure storage
  • No sensitive information like passwords is stored locally
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Regular security audits of our storage practices

Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
